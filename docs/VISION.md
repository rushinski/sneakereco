# SneakerEco — Product Vision

> **What it is (technical):** A multi-tenant white label vertical SaaS platform for sneaker resellers.  
> **What it is (plain English):** A service that builds you a fully custom website for your sneaker resale business — your brand, your domain, your identity. We build it, you own it, we stay invisible.

---

## The Problem

Sneaker resellers are real businesses. They move serious volume, they have real customers, and they need a real web presence. But the tools available to them were built for generic retail:

- **Shopify** gives you a template that looks like a thousand other stores, plasters their branding in your checkout, and has no concept of the sneaker industry. Worse — they have no underwriting. Resellers are classified as high-risk merchants. Sooner or later, Shopify will flag your account, freeze your funds, or shut you down for processing too much volume. There is no one to call.
- **Instagram** is where most resellers live — but it's a social feed, not a storefront. No search. No filtering. No checkout. No analytics. No SEO.
- **eBay and StockX** are channels, not brands. You're selling on their platform, building their audience, paying their fees.

The reseller has no home. No brand. No infrastructure built for how they actually operate.

SneakerEco is that infrastructure.

## Core Features

### Your Brand, End to End

Every storefront is built custom. No SneakerEco logo. No shared checkout page. No "Powered by" footer. Your domain (`heatkings.com`), your branded email (`orders@heatkings.com`, `support@heatkings.com`), your identity — entirely. We route your customer emails to whatever inbox you already use. You don't need to set up email infrastructure. You just need somewhere for the emails to land.

### Purpose-Built Payments (With Real Underwriting)

Sneaker resellers are high-risk merchants. Generic payment processors don't understand the business — they see large transactions, high volume, and unfamiliar merchant categories and they shut accounts down without warning. SneakerEco works with a payment gateway that provides proper merchant underwriting for resellers. We help you get approved. That means no account closures for doing too well.

The payment gateway handles card processing (credit, debit) and digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay). Beyond that, we support additional buy-now-pay-later and peer-to-peer payment options — Affirm, Afterpay, Klarna, Venmo, and Cash App — each requiring a separate application. We guide you through every approval process so you can offer your customers the widest range of payment options available.

### Invoicing

A lot of reseller business happens through DMs. A customer reaches out, you agree on a price, and then what — you tell them to Venmo you and hope they follow through? That creates friction, loses sales, and keeps everything off the books.

SneakerEco invoicing fixes this. You create an invoice with the products the customer wants, set the price, and send them a link. They land directly at checkout with those items loaded — no hunting around your site, no confusion. They pay using any available payment method, and if they're shipping, they'll receive automatic order updates without you having to send a single message.

The result is a faster close, a better customer experience, and every transaction living in your analytics alongside everything else. Your entire business — DMs included — consolidated in one place.

### Multi-Channel Listings, Kept in Sync

List once, sell everywhere. Connect your eBay and StockX accounts and post your inventory across all three channels simultaneously. When something sells on one platform, it's automatically pulled from the others. No overselling. No manual cleanup.

**Fee-aware pricing:** When you list on eBay, we know what eBay takes. If you want to net $100 on a sale, we'll list it at $105 (or whatever the math requires). You set the number you want to walk away with — we handle the markup. Guides are included to walk you through getting approved and connected for each channel.

### Instagram Sales Tracking

Most resellers close deals on Instagram DMs. That sale won't appear in any analytics tool — it's just a conversation. SneakerEco closes that gap: when you go to remove a product from your store after an Instagram sale, you'll be prompted to mark it as sold on Instagram and enter the price. That sale then flows into your analytics alongside everything else. One place for all your numbers.

### Smart Product Listing

Listing a product is usually slow. You write a title, find the right tags, describe the shoe, format the size — for every single item. SneakerEco maintains a catalog of brands, silhouettes, and colorways. Type your product name, upload your photos, enter size and cost. We do the rest: auto-tagging, SEO optimization, category assignment. The listing flow is fast by design, and the result is better — structured data means customers can actually find what they're looking for.

### Image Recentering

Product photos taken in the field are inconsistent — different angles, different distances, different backgrounds. SneakerEco's image recentering tool detects the shoe or product in the photo and recenters it so every listing looks uniform and professional. The shoe isn't modified — only its position in the frame. The result is a cohesive storefront that looks intentional, not improvised.

### Built-In SEO

Set your SEO preferences once. Every product listing you create is automatically optimized — structured metadata, proper tagging, search-friendly descriptions. You don't need to think about it. Over time, your store becomes discoverable on Google for the exact products you carry, building a sales channel that works without paid ads or social media.

### Market Data & Pricing Intelligence

Know what your inventory is worth before you list it. Market data is surfaced directly in the product posting flow, giving you real-time price context so you can list confidently. Price too high and it sits. Price too low and you leave money on the table. The data is there when you need it.

### In-Depth Analytics

Most resellers don't know their numbers. SneakerEco changes that. Track:

- Revenue by channel, time period, and product
- Conversion rates — how many visitors actually buy
- Stale listings — inventory that's been sitting too long
- Profit breakdowns — what you netted after fees and cost of goods
- Traffic sources — where your customers are coming from

The goal is to give you the insight to make better decisions about what to stock, how to price, and where to focus.

### Sales Tax & Fraud Decisioning

As volume grows, tax obligations follow. SneakerEco handles sales tax calculation and nexus tracking automatically. Orders are also run through fraud decisioning — protecting you from chargebacks and fraudulent purchases without you having to think about it.

### Standard Commerce, Done Right

Beyond the sneaker-specific features: product search and filtering, store sorting, customer accounts, order tracking, scheduled product drops, discounting (coming soon), and the full range of commerce capabilities your customers expect from a professional store.

## Platform Architecture (For Technical Context)

SneakerEco is a multi-tenant SaaS. Every reseller storefront is a tenant. All tenants share the same underlying infrastructure — API, database, frontend application — but their data is fully isolated at the database level via PostgreSQL Row-Level Security. From a reseller's perspective, they have their own website. From an infrastructure perspective, it's one platform serving many.

Tenant storefronts can run on SneakerEco subdomains (`heatkings.sneakereco.com`) or fully custom domains (`heatkings.com`) — the platform handles DNS, SSL, and routing transparently.

The platform is built on: NestJS (API), Next.js 16 App Router (frontend), PostgreSQL 18 + Drizzle ORM, AWS Cognito (auth), Valkey 9 + BullMQ (caching/queues), AWS SES (email), Cloudflare R2 (storage/CDN), Terraform (infrastructure), and Doppler (secrets). Deployed on DigitalOcean.

See `README.md` for developer setup and `MASTER_PLAN.md` for the full implementation specification.
